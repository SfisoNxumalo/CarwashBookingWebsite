Our Philosophy

“Cars don’t drive themselves to Johannes CAR WASH ™”. Franchise Office attributes their success to this simple philosophy of spoiling their customers and providing a superior service experience. Having established Johannes CAR WASH ™ as a strong and equitable brand in Gauteng the Franchise Office has recently spread their wings to Cape Town. Their team is busy securing future sites for this expansion and will also be looking to appoint Master Franchises in these areas.
